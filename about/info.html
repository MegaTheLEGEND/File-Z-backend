FAQ

Q: why is my game not loading?

A: the game you are trying to load may not (and probably never will) support "file://" protocol. (unity, ruffle-rs, emulators, etc.)


Q: Is File-Z Related to MATH.assist?

A: Yea. File-Z is the successor to MATH.assist. File-Z is still getting updated and improved, whereas MATH.assist has mostly been abandoned.


Q: Why does the list show some games but they dont exist?

A: This is probably due to the File-Z client not being up to date. Update File-Z.
